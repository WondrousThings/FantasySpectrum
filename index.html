Spectrum

You'll read a series of scenarios, one at a time, and rate how much each one appeals to you — from not interested to maximum interest. There are no right answers and nothing to explain. At the end, everything sorts by how it landed, and you can take a private summary anywhere you like.

Everyone in every scenario here is a consenting adult, and this is a private space to explore your own interests — there are no right answers. A few scenarios describe intense or higher-risk activities; strong interest in those is simply information about you, and anything you'd actually explore is worth learning to do safely and talking through openly with a partner. Nothing you enter ever leaves this device.
